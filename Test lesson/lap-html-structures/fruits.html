Fruits

The apple is crisp and sweet, perfect for a healthy snack. The orange, juicy and full of vitamin C, refreshes you instantly. A kiwi adds a tangy twist to any fruit salad. The banana, soft and full of energy, is ideal for breakfast. And the lychee, with its fragrant sweetness, is a tropical delight.

For a colorful fruit platter, include slices of apple and banana, some peeled orange segments, green kiwi halves, and fresh lychee. These fruits not only look great together but also offer a wide variety of nutrients and flavors that everyone will enjoy.

Whether blended into a smoothie or eaten fresh, fruits like apple, orange, kiwi, banana, and lychee make any meal more vibrant. They support your immune system, provide energy, and bring natural sweetness without added sugar.
